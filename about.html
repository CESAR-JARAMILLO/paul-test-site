Hi, I'm Paul!

I am a Latin American composer of music and lyrics, guitarist, vocalist, and bassist, focused on using loops to create and perform captivating experiences.

I have various influences, from Jazz to blues, from Pop to Groove, from classical to EDM, and use my variety of influences to craft a show that is virtuous and catchy in both English and Spanish.

I play covers and originals and can entertain a crowd for any length of time confidently, skillfully and professionally.

Being born and raised in the highly competitive music scene of Los Angeles California, I am familiar with meeting a hight standard of performance.
